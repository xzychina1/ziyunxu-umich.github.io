Case study

Atlas

A dashboard concept for keeping track of milestones, tasks, and project progress. The design aims for calm structure and quick visibility over dense information.

Back to portfolio See resume links

Challenge

The challenge was displaying several types of status information at once without creating visual noise or forcing too much scrolling.

Solution

I grouped related data into clear panels, used a restrained color palette, and kept the most important numbers close to the top of the page.

Role and tools

Information architecture, dashboard structure, and responsive layout with HTML and CSS.
